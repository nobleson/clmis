CONSTRUCTION LABOUR MARKET INFORMATION SYSTEM
(C-LMIS)

CONSTRUCTION LABOUR MARKET INFORMATION SYSTEM (C-LMIS) is a brand solution by CORBON. The C-LMIS platform is designed to meet the need for a solution that engages Artisans/Craftmen, labour, trade skills, Training Providers, Building and Construction, Companies and other shareholders for easy to quality labour and ensuring high standard in every activity within construction sector.

MISSION STATEMENT

To reduce skills gaps and shortages, improve productivity, increase opportunities for all individuals in the workforce and improve learning supply.
