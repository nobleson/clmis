The types of skills being demanded by employers do not always match those on offer by those who are looking for jobs. In order to tackle skills gap, the National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF) is used to increase the influence of stakeholders in the development of qualifications so that the system is more responsive to the needs of the labour market. Employers and workers' organisations have an important role to play in developing competency standards for qualifications.
